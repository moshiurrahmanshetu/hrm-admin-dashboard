AuraHR Leave Accrual Policies

Accruals Cycle
Annual Leave credits accrue on the first calendar day of every month. Unused annual leaves roll over up to a maximum of 10 days.
Medical Certification
Sick leave requests exceeding 2 consecutive working days require valid medical certification uploaded via attachments.
Half-Day Request Limits
Employees can submit up to 6 half-day casual leave requests per calendar quarter. Emergency contacts must be provided.
